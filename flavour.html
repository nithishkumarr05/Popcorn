Kettle-Crafted

Each batch is hand-popped in small copper kettles for optimal heat distribution and that signature fluffy-yet-crunchy texture.

Stone-Ground Spices

We grind our spice blends fresh weekly from whole seeds, chilies, and herbs — no pre-mixed powders.
